The origin of the idea

The world is opening up but the students from Bangladesh do not know how to get that opportunity. Often, students meet some agents who mislead and exploit them. As a result, students drop of of their course prematurely. This creates a problem for host universities/countires. At ASAP, we want to make sure that correct students get their resired course and the country of destination.

Out of that context, Dr. Golam Robbani has taken a step to open this service to genuine student with high quality service. Dr. Robbani is in full charge, therefore, there will be no chance to commit any fraud. You may know about Dr. Robbani's details in his linkedIn page
